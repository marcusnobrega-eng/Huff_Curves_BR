## Key international comparisons
- IETD sensitivity varies by climate regime — wet tropical shifts more events to Q1 (Water 2022, doi:10.3390/w14071047)
- Intensity threshold of 12.7 mm reinforces Q1 dominance in convective climates (Catena 2022, doi:10.1016/j.catena.2022.106371)
- Q1/Q2 dominance consistent finding in tropical/subtropical Asia, Malaysia, Korea, China
